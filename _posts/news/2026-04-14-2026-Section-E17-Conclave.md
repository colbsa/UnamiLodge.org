## What is Conclave?

Conclave is an exciting and engaging event where you’ll connect with fellow Arrowmen from across Pennsylvania, New Jersey, Delaware, and Puerto Rico. This section-wide gathering brings together lodges for a weekend of training, fellowship, and friendly competition.

Throughout Conclave, you’ll have access to a wide range of training sessions led by section leaders and special guests from the National OA. Beyond that, the weekend is packed with activities, games, and competitions designed for everyone!

## Theming:

This year’s theme, Revolutionary Fellowship, focuses on the celebration of the 250th anniversary of the founding of the United States of America. It’s a celebration of American culture, patriotism, revolutionary spirit, and US history. Overall, the weekend will be aimed towards fostering brotherhood against the backdrop of Scouts’ duty to country.

If you have any questions or concerns please reach out to the Unami Lodge Conclave Committee at conclave@UnamiLodge.org.
