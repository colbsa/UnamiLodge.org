The Community Service Day is our Lodge’s paramount way to give back to our local neighborhoods. Arrowmen, friends and family are all invited to join us March 21st at each of the following sites.  If you plan to attend at Pennypack Park, please sign up through this link and put "Unami Lodge Service Day" as how you found out about the event

## Mount Moriah Cemetery
- Location: [6201 Kingsessing Ave, Philadelphia, PA 19142](https://maps.app.goo.gl/zjERVwojTT1G59Qq6){:target="_blank"}
- What: Clearing of cemetery grounds
- Contact: Tim English (timengbsa@yahoo.com) 610-585-1432

## Pennypack Park
- Location: [Environmental Center 8600A Verree Rd, Philadelphia, PA 19115](https://maps.app.goo.gl/YLt1TXbZE6b6sXpq5){:target="_blank"}
- What: Working on putting up a fence
- Contact: Anton Meyer (anton.meyer@unamilodge.org) 267-622-3257

Check-in for the service projects will be between 8:30 AM and 9:00 AM at Mt Moriah and 9:30 AM at Pennypack Park with the projects being finished around noon. **Please wear appropriate attire for the work projects. Gloves and water are heavily recommended.** Lunch will not be provided, so individuals may pack a lunch or snack, or units that are attending together may have members volunteer to bring snacks. It would be a great help for any registered scouts or scouters to attend, and make sure you spread the word in your Chapter, Troop, Ship, or Crew! Remember that these events are open to everyone so encourage them to attend as well.

Questions? Reach out to the contact person for your service project or service@UnamiLodge.org.

Thank you,  
Service Committee
