## Get an Arrow ID Account
The Order of the Arrow uses a single sign-on system called Arrow ID to access official OA resources, including the OA Member Portal, LodgeMaster, and the national event registration system (NOARS). You must be a registered member of Scouting America in order of claim your account. 

If you have ever attended a NOAC or NLS/DYLC, you may already have an Arrow ID account.

<hr>

### Step 1: Create (or confirm) Your Arrow ID
Before requesting access to the OA Member Portal, <u>you must have an Arrow ID account.</u>

<ol>
  <li>Go to <a href="https://id.oa-scouting.org/Account/Register">id.oa-scouting.org/Account/Register</a></li>
  <li>Enter your name, email address, and desired password</li>
  <li>Click Register</li>
  <li>Follow any additional instructions to complete your registration</li>
</ol>

<i>If you already have an Arrow ID, you may skip this step.</i>

## Request Access to the OA Member Portal
If you do not yet have access to the OA Member Portal, Unami Lodge now provides a self-service request form to generate your Member Portal invitation.

### Step 2. Submit the Member Portal Access Request Form

<ol>
  <li>Go to <a href="https://workflow.unamilodge.org/form/portal-invite-request">Member Portal Invite Request</a></li>
  <li>Enter your Scouting Member ID and Date of Birth</li>
  <li>Click Submit</li>
</ol>

Once submitted, the system will send a Member Portal invitation email if you are eligible. 

<div class="col alert text-center alert-page alert-primary mb-0">
  NOTE: You must be 13 years of age or older to receive a Member Portal account. Parent access for members under 13 is not currently available.
</div>

<br><br>

## Complete Your Portal Setup
After receiving your invitation email: 

<ol>
  <li>Click on the link in the invitation email</li>
  <li>If prompted, log in using your Arrow ID</li>
  <li>If the account does not automatically link: </li>
    <ul>
     <li>Click the red “Have an invite?” button</li>
     <li>Select Member portal as the application</li>
     <li>Enter Council #525 - Cradle of Liberty</li>
     <li>Paste the invitation code from the email</li>
     <li>Click add</li>
    </ul>
</ol>
